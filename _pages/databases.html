I have built a number of databases to conduct my research. All will be available here, with associated documentation.

Lobbying and Policy Changes in EU Regulations (Available soon)

Dataset covering 482 Commission Regulations from 2015 to 2023, including official drafts and adopted texts. I also provide a tool to filter policy-relevant content and identify policy changes. 129,153 received comments on official drafts, linked to characteristics of respondents
