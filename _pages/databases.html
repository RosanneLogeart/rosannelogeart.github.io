I have built a number of databases to conduct my research. All will be available here, with associated documentation.

Lobbying and Policy Changes in EU Regulations (Available soon)

Dataset covering 482 Commission Regulations from 2015 to 2023, including official drafts and the final adopted policy texts. I also provide a tool to filter policy-relevant content in the drafts and final texts, and to identify policy changes occurring during the policy-making process.
The dataset also includes the 129,153 comments from interest groups made on these drafts during the policy-making process. I link each comment with the characteristics on the entity writting it.
This dataset is useful for researchers studying policy-making processes, as well as for policy-makers.
