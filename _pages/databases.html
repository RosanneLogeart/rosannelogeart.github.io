I have built a number of databases to conduct my research. All will be available here, with associated documentation.

Lobbying and Policy Changes in EU Regulations (Available soon)

Dataset covering 482 Commission Regulations from 2015 to 2023, including their unique official draft and the final adopted policy text. I also provide a tool to filter policy-relevant content from the drafts and final texts, and to identify policy changes occurring during the policy-making process.
The dataset includes the 129,153 comments made on these drafts by interest groups and citizens during the policy-making process.
This dataset is useful for researchers studying policy-making processes or influence dynamics.
