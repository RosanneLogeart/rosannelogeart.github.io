I have built a number of databases to conduct my research. All will be available here, with associated documentation.

Lobbying and Policy Changes in EU Regulations (Available soon)

Dataset covering 1,106 Commission Regulations from 2015 to 2025, including their unique official draft and final adopted policy text. I also provide a tool to filter policy-relevant content from drafts and final texts, and to identify policy changes between the draft and the final version of the text.
The dataset includes the 145,816 comments interest groups and citizens made on these drafts, before the elaboration of the final text.
This unique and comprehensive dataset is especially useful for researchers studying policy-making processes or the dynamics of influence.
